# Company Mission and Values

**Owner:** Executive Team
**Last updated:** 2025-06-01
**Status:** Foundational document, rarely changes

## Mission

Northwind Robotics exists to make warehouse work safer and more
efficient by building robots that work alongside people, not instead of
them. We are not trying to build "lights-out" fully automated warehouses
with no human workers — our product philosophy is collaborative
automation, where robots handle repetitive, physically demanding
transport tasks so warehouse staff can focus on higher-judgment work.

## Values

**Safety is non-negotiable.** Every employee, regardless of role, has the
standing authority to flag a safety concern and have it taken seriously,
no exceptions, no need to go through a chain of command first. See
Incident Response Process for how safety incidents specifically are
handled — they get elevated handling above our normal severity
framework.

**We ship and we measure.** We'd rather ship a smaller change and
measure its real-world impact than spend months theorizing about a
bigger one. This shows up in how we run engineering (see CI/CD Pipeline
Overview, Load Testing Guidelines) and product (see Beta Program
Guidelines, Customer Feedback Process).

**Blameless by default.** When something goes wrong, our first question
is "what about our system or process allowed this," not "who made the
mistake." See Incident Response Process for how this shows up concretely
in postmortems.

**Direct and kind.** We give each other direct feedback, including
feedback that's hard to hear, and we do it in a way that respects the
other person. See Manager Resources: 1:1 Guide and Code of Conduct for
how this is expected to show up day to day.

## How these values show up in this wiki

You'll notice these values referenced throughout other policy documents
— that's intentional. Policies at Northwind are meant to be traceable
back to these values, not arbitrary rules. If a policy ever seems to
contradict one of these values, that's worth raising with your manager
or People Team directly.
