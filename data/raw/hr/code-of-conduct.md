# Code of Conduct

**Owner:** People Team
**Last updated:** 2026-01-26
**Status:** Living document

This document describes code of conduct for Northwind Robotics employees.

## Overview

This policy applies company-wide unless otherwise noted, and is reviewed annually by the People Team.

## How it works in practice

Most employees interact with this process a handful of times during their tenure. Your manager is the first point of contact for questions; People Team is the second.

## Exceptions

Exceptions to standard policy are handled case by case and require People Team sign-off; there is no blanket self-service exception process.

## Notes
