# Remote Work Policy

**Owner:** People Team
**Last updated:** 2026-02-01
**Applies to:** All full-time employees in non-manufacturing roles

## Summary

Northwind Robotics operates on a hybrid model: employees in office-eligible
roles (engineering, product, G&A) are expected in their home office
location 3 days per week (Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday are the company-wide
anchor days), with flexibility to work remotely the other 2 days.
Warehouse operations, hardware lab, and facilities-dependent roles are
not eligible for this policy and follow site-specific schedules instead.

## Fully remote exceptions

A limited number of fully remote arrangements are approved on a
case-by-case basis, typically for specialized roles where the hiring pool
benefits significantly from geographic flexibility. Fully remote approval
requires director-level sign-off and is documented in the HR system. If
you have a fully remote arrangement, the 3-day anchor requirement does not
apply to you, but you are expected to travel to your assigned office for
quarterly team gatherings at company expense — see Travel Booking Policy.

## Working from outside your home country

Working remotely from a country other than your country of record requires
advance approval from both your manager and the People Team, generally at
least 3 weeks in advance, due to tax and legal employment considerations.
Unapproved extended work from another country can create real tax
liability for both you and the company, so this is one of the few areas
of this policy we enforce strictly rather than treating as a guideline.
Short trips (under 2 weeks, vacation-adjacent) generally don't require
this approval, but check with HR if you're unsure.

## Equipment and security

Remote work requires a Northwind-issued laptop (see Laptop Provisioning
Process) and an active VPN connection for accessing any internal systems
or customer data — see VPN Setup Guide. Personal devices may not be used
to access the fleet management systems or customer data under any
circumstances; see Device Security Requirements and BYOD Policy for the
limited exceptions (e.g. email/calendar) where personal device access is
permitted.
